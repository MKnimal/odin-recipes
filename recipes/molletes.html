Molletes

Description

If the culinary culture of the mexicans were a dish, the it would be molletes.
These little things are one of the easiest ways to cope with the hunger, and more times than you would think,
you will be able to make these with the leftovers in your kitchen

Ingredients:

  • Mexican bread type "bolillo"
  • Cheese type "chester" (or menonita cheese, as they say on my city)
  • Stewed beans
  • Buttercup

Steps:

  1. Slice the bread and apply a considerable amount of Buttercup on each side of the slice
  2. Toast the bread (preferably on a "comal" for better results)
  3. Spread the beans over the toasted bread
  4. Grate the cheese over the beans
  5. (Optional) Put it into the microwave for 30 seconds for better results
