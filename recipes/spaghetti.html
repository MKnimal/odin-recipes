Spaghetti

Description

This is what you make when you don't wanna cook in the rest of the day
The spaguetti has an special place on my hearth. Becuase, if it din't exist, i would had to learn how to cook,
and maybe I would have chosen the culinary school instead of IT.

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti paste
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Tomato sauce
  • Buttercup

Steps:

  1. Boil the water and add the salt
  2. When the water is boiling, add the Spaghetti paste and let it boil for 10 minutes
  3. Once the paste is boiled, put some Buttercup in a frying pan and melt it
  4. Then, add the tomato sauce and heat it until it starts to boil
  5. Add the Spaghetti paste to the tomato sauce and mix it for a minute
