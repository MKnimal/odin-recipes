Lemon Pie

Description

The old classic that almost every person in the world knows how to cook: a lemon pie.
You know that your aunt will bring this to every Christmas dinner, but that isn't a reason to deny how delicious it is.

Ingredients:

  • Lemon Juice
  • Condensed Milk
  • Evaporated milk
  • Cream
  • Pie base

Steps:

  1. First, mix the dairy products until they look homogeneous
  2. Then, add a tiny amount of juice until it has the right consistency
  3. After that, pour the mixture into the base
  4. At last, refrigerate for 2 hours
